How to use the Arithmetic Simplifier

  • To use the Arithmetic Simplifier, enter a mathematical expression in the input field and click the "Simplify" button. The result will appear in the "Results" section below.
  • The Arithmetic Simplifier supports addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, parentheses for grouping, and exponentiation using the caret symbol (^).
  • Invalid expressions or those with unsupported operations will display an error message.
  • Give the Arithmetic Simplifier a try and simplify your math expressions!
