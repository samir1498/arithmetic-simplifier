How to use the Math Solver

  • To use the Math Solver, enter a math expression in the input field and click the "Solve" button. The result will appear in the "Results" section below.
  • The Math Solver supports addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, parentheses for grouping, and exponentiation using the caret symbol (^).
  • Invalid expressions or those with unsupported operations will display an error message.
  • Give the Math Solver a try and simplify your math problems!
